What we’re building

A compact, fixed-geometry capture system designed to behave consistently across sessions and environments, enabling reliable downstream spatial viewing and evaluation.

Fixed geometry baseline

A controlled physical baseline reduces variability and improves repeatability.

Calibration as prerequisite

Calibration is treated as a controlled condition rather than an operator-dependent step.

Validation-driven iteration

Build, test, measure, refine. Progress is guided by validation, not assumptions.
